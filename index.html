About me

I grew up in Mexico City and moved with my three sons to Berlin in 2015. I started a stationery business because I believe in the strength of the written word and profoundly enjoy the design process with actual Gouache paint. While doing so, all I think of are colors, stripes, dots, and composition. I only think about doing something pretty, and I'd like to share this joy with those who buy my designs.

Why to purchase?


Since my products have graphics and symbols but no words, they can be purchased by anyone regardless of their language.
Because I love my planet, my products are environmentally friendly, with papers FSC-certified, recycled, and printed climate-neutral. Also, for parts of the production process, like packaging and bookbinding, I collaborate with USE gGmbH. This company creates enticing and sustainable work, education, and employment opportunities for disabled and disadvantaged people in Berlin.

Are these products hand painted? 

They are designed with actual Gouache paint by hand, which gives them a different texture and colour, but the final product has been printed like any other.

As I like to improve, future products will be produced even more environmentally friendly.
